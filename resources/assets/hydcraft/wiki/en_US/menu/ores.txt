Hydraulicraft adds two ores:
- Copper
- Lead
Apart from that it also generates [link{hydcraft:menu/minerals.txt}]minerals [link{}]

[image{200,90,0.27,hydcraft:textures/wiki/copper.png}]

Copper generates from y [variable{hydcraft:config:i:worldgen:copperMinY}]
to [variable{hydcraft:config:i:worldgen:copperMaxY}]
